Welcome to Discover's documentation!
====================================
Discover, or Distributed Sensing & Computing Over Sparse Environments, is
a platform allowing researchers to study distributed sensing in rural areas.
This documentation will provide users with an in-depth explanation of the
Discover Portal, which you will use to create, submit, and examine your
experiments, along with tutorials on how to use the portal and our hardware.
